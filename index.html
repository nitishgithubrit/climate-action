Who We Are

The Climate Heroes is a club registered at the Technical University of Kenya. Comprised of passionate youth, we are committed to contributing to the United Nations SDG 13 - Climate Action. Through sensitization, informative webinars, and community outreach programs, we aim to take consistent actions to make our planet a better place.

"You are the change you've been waiting for, so take a daring step and go beyond, for you're capable!"

Barrack Obama, Former USA President

Join us at the Technical University of Kenya and be part of the change. Together, we can make a difference.

What We've Done

Take a look at some of our accomplishments:

1,246 Trees Planted

We have planted 1,246 trees in various institutions within Nairobi and nearby cities. Trees absorb carbon dioxide, helping to mitigate climate change.

City Clean-up

We organize regular city clean-up events, encouraging community members to take responsibility for their environment and maintain cleanliness.

Training Programs

We provide training programs to educate young people, empowering them to think innovatively and bring solutions to combat climate change.
